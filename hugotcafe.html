About:

#Hugot has gripped the country as of late. Whenever someone posts something on social media, people are quick to make that post be associated with relationship issues, usually in the form of something heart-breaking. One café in Tondo, Manila has taken advantage and have made a living off of these #hugotlines: Hugot Café. .
