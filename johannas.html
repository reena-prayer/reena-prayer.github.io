Johanna's Grille



ABOUT:

Johanna's Grille restaurant is homely but dainty setting. We serve one fine sophisticated dish that would be our famous trademark - baby back ribs.

Baby back ribs are made from a specific exquisite part of the pork cooked to "slide-off-the-bone" tenderness, and grilled to perfection with hickory sauce and premium spices. For Avid customers of Johanna's Grille, this description becomes an understatement.

The restaurant's menu also features a wide variety of equally delectable house specials, with most customers preferring the all-time filipino favorite crispy pata, sizzling long, tacos, crispy liempo, Paella fried rice ,callos. The place is ideal for family and friends' get-together for birthdays, anniversaries, or just about any celebration.

For food lovers, and Enthusiasts, take a hold of our Johanna's Grille menu, and adventurously seek out an entree from the list that will definitely satisfy your heart and stomach.

Make Johanna's baby back ribs a delicious headstart.
