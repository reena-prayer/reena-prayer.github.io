About:

He Brews Cafe is a coffee shop and restaurant in Batangas City.They serve breakfast, lunch, dinner, hot & cold coffee and a variety of drinks. They also offer J. Co donuts for everyone to enjoy! Visit their store for some refreshments from Monday to Sunday mostly 10:00AM to 8:00PM. You can dine inside their cafe or take out your orders. As of now, they only accept cash payments. Currently, He Brews Cafe - Batangas City is being managed by Ms. Janeth Mercado.
