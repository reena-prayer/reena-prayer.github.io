About:

From their homemade pizza dough & fire roasted vegetables, to those legendary signature dishes, all of their food is handcrafted and prepared fresh throughout the day. They offer Italian cuisine. They also serves lunch, dinner, coffee and drinks. The restaurant was founded in 2012 and managed by Deo de Castro.
