OUR STORY

The FoodHUB originally started as a simple idea in 2016 at Batangas State University. We recognized the hunger to find the newest, one of a kind nor the top restaurants who made the cut and made a long lasting impression to Batanguenos' delicate taste. While making the most of Batangas province, we started a little website to provide some helpful information for food lovers to fulfill the desires of their heart that may went way beyond their expectations. We felt empowered and proud to learn and discover Batangas' rich food culture and share to the rest of the world.
